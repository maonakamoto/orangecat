# OrangeCat Strategic Business Plan

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025
**Document Owner:** Founder & CEO
**Classification:** Executive Confidential
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly

## 📋 Executive Summary

OrangeCat is building the infrastructure for personal economies and voluntary social support in the Bitcoin era, powered by AI. We provide a comprehensive platform where individuals can monetize skills, share resources voluntarily, and support community welfare through direct Bitcoin transactions, with personalized AI assistants that understand each user's complete ecosystem of economic activities and social contributions.

**Current Status:** MVP launched with AI assistant functionality, early user testing
**Funding Status:** Bootstrapped solo development
**Dual Value Proposition:** AI-powered platform for both personal economic participation and voluntary social welfare

### 2028+ Objectives (Long-term)

- **Global Impact:** Enable widespread economic participation through AI assistance

## 📊 Market Analysis

### Market Opportunity

- **Target Market:** Professionals seeking to monetize expertise with AI assistance
- **Market Gap:** No existing platform combines AI personal assistants with Bitcoin payments for comprehensive personal economies
- **Early Adopter Focus:** Bitcoin-curious professionals in high-value fields

### Target Segments

1. **Professional Services:** Lawyers, doctors, consultants, engineers seeking AI-powered practice management
2. **Creative Economy:** Artists, writers, designers, content creators needing AI marketing and business support
3. **Social Welfare:** Community members providing voluntary support for education, healthcare, and mutual aid
4. **Entrepreneurial:** Startup founders, researchers, innovators requiring AI strategic assistance

### Competitive Landscape

- **AI Platforms:** Generic AI assistants without economic/financial integration
- **Bitcoin Platforms:** Payment-focused without AI personalization or comprehensive economic management
- **Professional Tools:** Specialized software lacking integrated AI and Bitcoin capabilities
- **Differentiation:** Only platform offering AI assistants aware of complete personal economic ecosystems with Bitcoin payments

## 💰 Business Model

> Updated 2026-07-02 per `master-plan-2026-07.md` §3.3 — "sell intelligence,
> not rails". The previous transaction-fee model contradicted both the
> non-custodial legal posture (the platform never processes or holds funds)
> and the public 0%-fee positioning, and is retired.

### The model: 0% rails, paid intelligence

1. **Peer-to-peer payments: 0% platform fees, forever, non-custodial.**
   Payments settle wallet-to-wallet (NWC / Lightning address / on-chain);
   OrangeCat never touches the funds. This is the marketing wedge AND the
   legal posture — never reintroduce a transaction take-rate.
2. **Cat Credits (built, primary revenue):** prepaid Lightning top-ups spent
   on platform-served frontier AI, billed at provider cost + 20% margin
   (SSOT: `src/services/cat/credit-metering.ts`). Selling our own service —
   prepaid credits — not payment processing.
3. **Paid AI assistants (built):** creators charge per message; the platform
   keeps 5% of the AI-service price (95% creator share). The only take-rate,
   and it is on platform-mediated AI services, not P2P money.
4. **Supporter subscription (future):** a flat CHF/month plan (proposal:
   CHF 10) with a monthly frontier allowance. Deliberately NOT advertised
   until it can actually be purchased.
5. **Professional services / data insights:** long-horizon options, not
   planned revenue.

### Cost Structure

- **Technology:** AI infrastructure, cloud services, development tools
- **Operations:** Customer support and community management
- **Marketing:** Content creation and community building
- **Legal & Compliance:** Regulatory monitoring and legal counsel

### Business Focus

- **Bootstrapped Growth:** Sustainable growth without external funding
- **Value-Driven Pricing:** Fees that provide clear value to users
- **Network Effects:** Platform value increases with more users and data

## 🚀 Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Current)

- **Target:** Early adopters interested in AI + Bitcoin integration
- **Channels:** Bitcoin communities, tech forums, professional networks
- **Goals:** Establish user base, validate AI assistant value proposition

### Phase 2: AI-assisted builders (primary ICP — updated 2026-07-02)

- **Target:** Solo builders and micro-studios shipping with AI agents —
  FleetCrown's exact audience; people who need a public economic presence
  without a company, a Stripe account, or a real name
- **Channels:** FleetCrown cross-sell (identity bridge, live since
  2026-07-02), founder's build-in-public loop, Swiss Bitcoin community
- **Goals:** Closed loops — published offers, completed payments, credit
  spend. (The earlier lawyers/doctors persona set is retired; it was
  AI-generated fiction never validated against a real user.)

### Phase 3: Market Expansion

- **Target:** Broader creative and pseudonymous-builder communities
- **Channels:** Partnerships, referrals, content-driven growth, SEO from
  server-rendered public entity pages
- **Goals:** Scale adoption, refine AI capabilities based on user feedback

## 📈 Key Risks & Mitigation

### AI & Technology Risks

- **AI accuracy and bias:** Rigorous testing and user feedback loops for AI responses
- **Data privacy:** Strong privacy controls and transparent data usage policies
- **AI dependency:** Maintaining human oversight and fallback capabilities

### Bitcoin & Regulatory Risks

- **Bitcoin volatility:** User education on cryptocurrency fundamentals
- **Regulatory uncertainty:** Monitoring regulatory developments and maintaining compliance
- **Payment reliability:** Robust error handling and user communication

### Business & Market Risks

- **User adoption:** Focus on clear value proposition and professional use cases
- **Competition:** Differentiated AI + Bitcoin integration as barrier to entry
- **Market timing:** Gradual education of professional segments on platform benefits
